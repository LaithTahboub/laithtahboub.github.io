  2. LLM alignment to normative text corpora with Professor Mohammed Nayeem Teli
    • December 2024 – June 2025
    • I led a cross-functional team of 5 undergraduate researchers to build an evaluation pipeline for measuring how well large language models align with normative text corpora. My responsibilities included coordinating experiments across the team and managing our workflows on the university's high-performance computing cluster.
    • I developed a novel evaluation method that generates over 100,000 question-answer pairs from philosophical, legal, and religious texts. We used these pairs to benchmark leading LLMs including GPT-4, Claude, and Llama 3, implementing our pipeline with PyTorch, HuggingFace, PEFT, and SLURM.
    • Our key finding was that top LLMs are consistently more factually aligned than morally aligned with these normative texts.

  3. Predicting Professor Ratings
    • February 2025
